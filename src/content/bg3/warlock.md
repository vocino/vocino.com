> **Starter notes.** This is my default Warlock, shared as a starting point rather than a verdict. Tune the invocations and pact to taste.

A **Fiend** Warlock built around the most reliable damage in the game: an Agonizing, Repelling [[bg3:eldritch-blast]]. Short-rest slots mean I never feel poor, [[bg3:hex]] tops up every beam, and Repelling Blast turns any cliff into an instant-kill.

## Why I run it

- **Eldritch Blast scales for free** — multiple beams, each with CHA damage added.
- **Repelling Blast** shoves enemies off heights and into hazards.
- **Short-rest recovery** keeps my best slots available every fight.

## Feats I take

- Ability Improvement (Charisma) — more beam damage and DCs.
- [[bg3:war-caster|War Caster]] — hold concentration on [[bg3:hex]] through hits.

## Spells & actions

- [[bg3:eldritch-blast|Eldritch Blast]] — the entire build, every turn.
- [[bg3:hex|Hex]] — bonus-action damage rider that follows kills to new targets.
- [[bg3:armor-of-agathys|Armor of Agathys]] — punishes anything that melees me.
- [[bg3:counterspell|Counterspell]] — upcast for free off a short rest.

## Gear by act

- **Act 1:** Items that add a flat bonus to cantrip damage.
- **Act 2:** Gear that boosts spell save DC.
- **Act 3:** A staff that grants an extra reaction or bonus blast charge.

## How I play it

Bonus-action [[bg3:hex]] on the priority target, then fire [[bg3:eldritch-blast]] every turn. If an enemy is near a drop, I aim Repelling Blast to shove it off instead of chipping HP. When Hex's target dies, I move the curse to the next threat on my bonus action.

## Make it yours

Want melee? **Pact of the Blade** plus a couple **Paladin** levels makes a charisma-SAD smiting hybrid. Prefer utility? **The Great Old One** with Pact of the Chain gives you a permanent invisible scout. The blast core never changes.
